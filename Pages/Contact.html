Common questions

Quick answers to the things NGO teams ask us most often.

  • Can you onboard our whole staff at once?
    Yes. We can coordinate cohort-based onboarding for multiple teams and regions at the same time.
  • Do you offer custom training pathways?
    We can combine modules from different programs to match your context and existing initiatives.
  • Will our team receive certificates?
    Learners who complete all modules in a program will receive a shareable certificate.
  • Can we access trainings offline?
    Some resources can be downloaded for offline facilitation. Reach out to discuss your connectivity constraints.
